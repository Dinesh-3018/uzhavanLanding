How it works

The UZHAVAN project connects farmers and buyers directly. Farmers list their products online. Buyers place orders, and farmers fulfill them. Products are delivered to buyers, creating a transparent and efficient system

Farmers Join

Farmers join the platform, providing details about their products and practices

Direct Ordering

Consumers browse products online, place orders, and pay directly to farmerS

Preparation

Farmers harvest and prepare orders, ensuring quality and freshness.

Delivery

Products are either collected by consumers or delivered to their doorsteps, creating a direct link between farmers and consumers while promoting transparency and efficiency.
